## How to add a publication to the website

1. Add the entry to the correct table above
2. Open `index.html`, find `<!-- PUBLICATIONS SECTION -->`
3. Copy a `<div class="pub-item">` block, paste at the top, and update:
   - `.pub-year` — year
   - `.pub-title` — paper title
   - `.pub-authors` — author list
   - `.pub-venue` — journal/conference name
   - `.pub-link href` — URL to PDF or DOI
