What we do

Research Focus Areas

Our work spans theoretical foundations to real-world systems, tackling open problems in communications, signal processing, and machine learning.

📡
Wireless Communications
Channel modeling, MIMO systems, massive antenna arrays, and next-generation 5G/6G transceiver design.
🌊
Adaptive Signal Processing
Sparse signal recovery, compressed sensing, adaptive filtering, and time-frequency analysis.
🤖
Machine Learning for Communications
Deep learning-based channel estimation, end-to-end learning of communication systems, and AI-driven resource allocation.
🎯
Radar & Sensing
FMCW radar signal processing, integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), and target detection algorithms.
🔐
Physical Layer Security
Secure beamforming, covert communication, and information-theoretic security for wireless networks.
🔊
Audio & Speech Processing
Speech enhancement, noise cancellation, acoustic beamforming, and speaker recognition systems.
