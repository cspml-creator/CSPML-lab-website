# News / Event Photos

Place photos for news articles and announcements here.

## Recommended Specifications
- **Size:** 1000 × 500 pixels (2:1 landscape ratio)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **File naming:** Date + short title in lowercase
  - Example: `2025-04-award-spcom.jpg`, `2025-01-serb-grant.jpg`

## How to Use
Find the news card in `index.html`:

```html
<!-- EDIT: NEWS FEATURED IMAGE → replace src below -->
<img class="news-featured-img" src="images/placeholder/news.svg"
     alt="Best Paper Award at SPCOM 2024">
```

Change to your photo:

```html
<img class="news-featured-img" src="images/news/2024-spcom-award.jpg"
     alt="Best Paper Award at SPCOM 2024">
```

## Suggested Photos
- Conference presentations / poster sessions
- Award ceremonies
- Lab visits / inaugurations
- Team outings / group photos from events
